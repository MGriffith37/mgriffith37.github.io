About Me

An Introduction

Welcome to the bit about me.

When I started my Engineering degree in 2015 at the University of Auckland, I had no idea what I would end up as. Looking back now, an education in Software Engineering was staring me in the face all along.

I've always enjoyed tinkering with computers; installing exciting apps, testing them to the limit, fiddling with the settings, playing games, etc. Even the terrifying myth of the "square eyes" couldn't quell my curiosity. And now that I'm able to tinker away, learn new and impressive ways to use a computer and get a career off of it? I couldn't be happier!

In 2016, I started studying Software Engineering as a specialisation.
This introduced me to:

  • Object-Oriented Programming with Java
  • Data Algorithms
  • Linux Bash
  • Quality Assurance

Recently, I've focused on front-end web development with:

  • HTML/CSS/JS
  • React
