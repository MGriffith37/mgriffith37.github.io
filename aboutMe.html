About Me

An Introduction

Welcome to the bit about me.

When I started my Engineering degree in 2015 at the University of Auckland, I had no idea what I would end up as. Looking back now, an education in Software Engineering was staring me in the face all along.

I've always enjoyed working with computers; installing exciting apps, testing them to the limit, fiddling with the settings, playing games, etc. Even the terrifying prospect of getting "square eyes" couldn't quell my curiosity. And now that I'm able to have a career where I can tinker away and learn new, impressive ways to use a computer? I couldn't be happier!

In 2016, I started studying Software Engineering as a specialisation.
This introduced me to:

  • Object-Oriented Programming with Java
  • Data Algorithms
  • Linux Bash
  • Quality Assurance

Recently, I've focused on front-end web development with:

  • HTML/CSS/JS
  • React
