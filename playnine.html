Play Nine

An interactive React game

The Rules

The goal of Play Nine is to use each number only once during the game, without running out of options to complete a turn.

Every turn, you are allocated a random amount of stars. Select the number or numbers that sum to the amount of stars, then click the equal sign. A correct answer will turn the button green and you can click it again to submit. You can withdaw numbers from your answer by clicking them.

If you are unable to select enough stars to equal the required amount, you can click the orange redraw button for a new set of stars. Be careful though! You only get 5 redraws per game. Good luck!
